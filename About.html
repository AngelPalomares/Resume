About Me

Hello, I'm Angel Martin Palomares, a dedicated individual with a background in Digital Media and Digital Gaming & Simulation. Born in Mexico and raised in Houston, Texas, I've shaped my academic journey with a Masters of Science from Drexel University and a Bachelors of Science from the University of Houston Victoria.

My lifelong passion for game creation became evident early on. Starting with crafting Game Design Documents for the Pikmin series during my childhood, I envisioned enemies, mechanics, and playable characters. Upon entering university, I resolved to pursue a career as a software engineer, driven by the desire to bring to life the games that have always captivated me.

With a background enriched by academic achievements and diverse experiences, I've had the privilege of working as a software engineer with several companies. I'm motivated to continue growing, learning, and contributing to the dynamic world of technology and game development.
