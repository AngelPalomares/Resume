About Me

In the world of digital creation, where code meets creativity, I've found my calling. I'm Angel, a game developer and software engineer with a penchant for turning complex problems into engaging, interactive experiences. Born from a passion for gaming and a curiosity for programming, my journey has been one of constant learning, innovation, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in digital media.

My Adventure in Tech

From the bustling city of Houston to the historic streets of Philadelphia, my career has been a journey through the heart of game development and software engineering. As the Lead Software Engineer at Gamucatex, I've been at the forefront of developing cutting-edge gaming features, including pioneering a C#-based save system that redefined our approach to player interaction in WebGL environments.

At Code Wiz and Drexel University, I shared my knowledge and passion for game development with eager minds, teaching the next generation of developers the principles of programming, UI/UX design, and animation in Unity. These experiences not only deepened my understanding of game development but also underscored the importance of mentorship in the tech industry.

The Tech Toolkit

•Game Development Tools: Unity Engine, Godot Engine, Unreal Engine

But beyond the code, it's the skills of leadership, problem-solving, and the ability to coach and develop teams that truly define my professional persona.

A Journey of Continuous Learning

The pursuit of knowledge took me to the University of Houston Victoria, where I laid the foundation of my career with a Bachelor of Science in Digital Gaming and Simulation, and later to Drexel University, where I earned my Master of Science in Digital Media. These academic milestones were not just about earning degrees; they were about crafting a vision for the kind of developer I wanted to become.
