Conquer Your Alcoholism

Unity 2D

This game is designed to be a serious experience. The narrative unfolds as you emerge from rehabilitation, presented with a final opportunity at your current workplace. The objective is to survive while resisting the temptation of alcohol; losing all your money results in a game over.

  • Lead Programer.
  • Enhanced gameplay with vital features, including a robust health system, fluid movement mechanics, an engaging boss battle system, and a dynamic collectible system featuring valuable items such as currency and health pickups.
  • Developed a dynamic system that simulates in-game intoxication by progressively blurring the player's screen with each hit. As the number of hits increases, the blurriness intensifies, accompanied by a gradual reduction in the player's movement speed. Upon reaching the maximum number of hits, the control scheme undergoes a switch, providing an immersive and challenging gaming experience.
